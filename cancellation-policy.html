This Cancellation Policy outlines the parameters for stopping active services or cancelling software projects with Smmpayjust InfoTech (Owner: Vasudha R).

1. Prior to Project Commencement

Customers may request project cancellation at any time before our team begins design wireframing or software coding. If no labor hours have been logged or server space booked, a full cancellation will be approved and payments returned as defined in our Refund Policy.

2. After Project Commencement

Once design wireframing, layout engineering, or backend programming has started, project cancellation requests will incur charges. These charges are computed based on:

  • The project milestone parameters completed.
  • Labor hours logged by our developers and designers.
  • Expenses incurred on third-party licenses, cloud directories, or domains.

3. Auto-Cancellation on Non-Payment

We reserve the right to suspend or cancel active development if milestone payments are delayed by more than 15 calendar days from the invoice date. Re-activating a suspended project may incur setup charges.

4. How to Submit a Cancellation Request

To request project cancellation, email us directly at contact@smmpayjustinfotech.com or speak with your assigned account manager. You must include your invoice number and project details.
