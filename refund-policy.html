At Smmpayjust InfoTech (Owner: Vasudha R), we strive to deliver top-tier custom software development, website designs, and digital marketing setups. Because our deliverables are digital and service-based, our refund parameters are governed by the following rules:

1. Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility depends entirely on the specific service agreement and project milestones signed between the client and Smmpayjust InfoTech. All requests are evaluated individually by our management team.

2. Custom Website & Software Work

Custom website design, database programming, and software development projects represent dedicated labor hours and infrastructure bookings. Therefore, development work is generally non-refundable once design layout prototyping or active coding has started.

3. Case-by-Case Reviews

If you wish to request a project cancellation or payment adjustment, you must submit a written request outlining your reasons. All refund claims will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis by analyzing work hours logged, wireframes delivered, and project milestones completed.

4. Processing Timelines

If a refund request is approved by our management team, the transaction will be processed and credited back to the customer's account within 7 to 10 business days. Refunds are executed through the original payment method (e.g., credit card, UPI, netbanking) utilized during payment processing on Razorpay.

5. Non-Refundable Items

Unless explicitly agreed in writing, no refunds will be issued for:

  • Completed digital services, launched portals, and handed-over source files.
  • Consulting hours, design meetings, or custom strategy research.
  • Third-party purchases made on behalf of the client (such as domain names, cloud hosting plans, premium plugins, licensing modules, or APIs).

6. Contact Us
