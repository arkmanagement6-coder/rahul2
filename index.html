FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers regarding timelines, payments, technologies, and post-launch support services.

What services does Smmpayjust InfoTech offer?

We specialize in Website Development (Business, Corporate, Ecommerce), Custom software (CRM, ERP, Portal solutions), School/Hospital Management platforms, Digital Marketing (SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads), Domain & Hosting setup, and API Payment Gateway integrations.

How long does it take to build a basic business website?

Typically, a standard 5-to-10 page business website takes around 1 to 2 weeks, depending on requirement scopes and visual design layouts approval speed.

Do you offer domain names and cloud hosting?

Yes, we provide domain registration and cloud server setup services. We set up fast, reliable hosting environments with global CDNs and security certificates.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and responsive?

Absolutely. All websites we build are 100% responsive and tested across iOS, Android, and Desktop sizes to ensure a premium visual appearance.

Do you provide SEO optimization services?

Yes, we offer complete On-Page SEO (meta tags, descriptions, schema markups, speed, canonical urls) and custom Off-Page SEO optimization campaigns to rank your products.

What payment methods do you support?

We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, credit cards, debit cards, and corporate payments handled securely through Razorpay and standard payment gateways.

Is there a refund policy if a project is cancelled?

Refund eligibility details are specified in our Refund Policy. Custom development work is non-refundable once design or code development starts. Inquiries are evaluated case-by-case.

Do you integrate payment gateways like Razorpay?

Yes, we specialize in payment gateway integrations (Razorpay, Stripe, Paytm) with support for subscriptions, auto-invoices, and split payments.

Do you sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA)?

Yes, we prioritize customer IP and data privacy. We sign legal NDAs with enterprises and startups before gathering project details.

Do you offer website maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer quarterly and monthly website maintenance plans covering code updates, security patches, performance audits, and database backups.

Can I update my website content easily?

Yes, we integrate easy-to-use Content Management Systems (CMS) like WordPress, Shopify, or custom admin control panels, so you can edit text and images without coding.

What frameworks do you use for development?

We work primarily with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Next.js, PHP, Laravel, Node.js, and MySQL. We select the stack based on security, speed, and scaling parameters.

How do you ensure website data security?

We implement SSL certificates, secure hosting directories, cross-site scripting (XSS) prevention, database sanitation protocols, and periodic backups.

Do you support custom CRM or ERP integrations?

Yes, we build APIs or map webhooks to link your ERP/CRM software with existing accounting, HR, or scheduling services.

How do I get started with Smmpayjust InfoTech?

Simply click on our "Get Free Quote" or "Contact Us" buttons, fill in details of your project parameters, and a representative will connect with you in 24 hours.
