This Shipping & Delivery Policy defines how deliverables and service codes are transferred from Smmpayjust InfoTech (Owner: Vasudha R) to our clients.

1. Digital-Only Services

Smmpayjust InfoTech specializes in website development, custom software design, database configurations, and online marketing. We provide digital services only. No physical products are shipped or delivered.

2. Delivery Methods

All project deliverables, including source codes, system database configurations, design wireframes, analytics reports, and other digital assets, are delivered electronically. The transfer is handled through:

  • Direct emails containing secure file attachments.
  • Secure cloud storage download folders (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox).
  • Version control system links (e.g., GitHub, GitLab repositories).
  • Client portal credentials and cloud server administrator access panels.

3. Delivery Timelines

The timeline for completing and delivering digital deliverables depends entirely on the selected service and project scope parameters. Milestones and delivery schedules are agreed upon in writing before work begins. Standard website launches usually range from 1 to 3 weeks, whereas custom CRM/ERP projects may require longer timelines.

4. Shipping Costs

Since all deliverables are transferred electronically, there are no shipping charges, packaging costs, or logistics fees applicable to our services.
